01  —  The Big Idea特撮 · The world of the Catronauts

Where does the
flavour come from?

Ki has a live-action universe — the tokusatsu (特撮, “special filming”) world of the Catronauts. This series extends that same world to answer a simple brand question with fantastical imagery: where does the flavour come from? Each piece dramatises a flavour’s reason-to-believe as a scene from that universe — not a product shot, but a piece of world.

Five fantastical “ingredient” scenes, each a true frame of the Catronaut tokusatsu world — each making a flavour feel inevitable rather than invented.
The one-line brief

03
03  —  The World RulesApprove before scenes 2–5

Tokusatsu is
the spine

Tokusatsu is a Japanese live-action tradition built on tangible, practical effects — real sets, miniatures, suits and in-camera craft over CGI. We borrow its soul, not any single franchise. Use it as a toolkit; never reproduce a recognisable existing property.

The genre toolkit — pull from these, blend freely
怪獣
Kaiju

Giant, awe-inspiring creatures. An ingredient at monstrous scale — a fruit the size of a hill, a guardian beast of the orchard.

変身
Henshin heroes

Transforming, masked figures. The moment an ingredient “powers up” — a ritual, a device, a transformation that yields the flavour.

戦隊
Super Sentai · teams

Colour-coded teams with signature gear and mecha. The five flavours read as a coordinated set — each its colourway, all one squad.

巨大
Kyodai heroes

Heroes who grow giant to meet a kaiju. Human-scale stewards (or robots) among colossal ingredients — the satsuma harvesters are this.

Non-negotiable look-and-feel
  • 01Practical over digital.Tangible, in-camera craft — suits, miniatures, real light, real dust. The uncanny should feel built, not rendered.
  • 02Real ingredient, fantastical frame.The flavour’s actual fruit or botanical is always real and accurate; only the world around it is heightened.
  • 03Cinematic, golden, grounded.Filmic light (the satsuma sunset is the benchmark), shallow depth, a sense of place in Japan.
  • 04Action and heart.Behind the spectacle, a quiet human warmth — stewardship, ritual, care. Ki is “a gift of presence,” not a battle.
  • 05One flavour per image.Never blend flavours, pucks or palettes in a single scene.

06  —  Brand GuardrailsNon-negotiable

The lines we
don’t cross

Logo

The square lockup only

The letters KI with the kanji 気 in a dark square. Never a bare “KI” wordmark, never redrawn — composite the real asset.

Japanese

The only kanji is 気

Never invent kanji or Japanese text. Flavour Japanese names come only from the real label, copied exactly.

Type

Obviously, everywhere

Obviously is the brand face — no serif anywhere in the work.

Colour

Flavour-keyed palettes

Palettes come from the packaging, one flavour at a time. No sepia or vintage wash over the whole frame.

Colour · the #1 drift

Absolutely no blue

No indigo, navy, teal or neon — this is the most common drift. The world stays in flavour-keyed, golden, grounded colour.

07  —  Legal · Test The Whole Kit FirstA gate, not a formality

Treat legal
as a gate

These are exploratory, “weird” motifs on a nicotine product. Per prior legal guidance, anything in this register can trigger liability if shown outside the 18+ age gate or anywhere near regulated packaging.

18+ · UK
Warning: Contains nicotine. Nicotine is an addictive chemical.
Behind the age gate·Never on product·Never on the open site
  • 01Age gate.Everything in this series lives behind the 18+ gate on the website. Never on product, never on the open site, never on a press-ready label.
  • 02No IP collisions.Borrow the tokusatsu genre, never a recognisable franchise, suit, monster or hero design. Original creatures and devices only.
  • 03No health claims.RTB is about flavour origin, not benefit. Nothing that implies wellness, safety or efficacy.
  • 04Warning + 18+.Any product-facing frame carries the required nicotine warning; no imagery that could read as appealing to minors.
  • 05Test as one package.Hold publication until the full kit — 5 images + puck sets + lines — is reviewed together, so legal sees the world, not isolated frames.
